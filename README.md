# kubejs

Tech modpack featuring Create, Immersive Engineering & Ad Astra. Journey across the solar system and beyond as you advance through the ages of human technology, from flint tools and crude furnaces to integrated circuits and interstellar rockets.

## Features
- Curated progression through 5+ technology tiers
- Comprehensive quest book with unique rewards
- Custom ore processing and chemistry based on [Immersive Geology](github.com/Immersive-Geology-Team/Immersive-Geology/)
- Non-linear progression options - multiple ways to produce or process one resource
- Hundreds of custom recipes and many custom materials

This pack is still very much in development and not fit for any kind of release.

## Content Mods
- **Create** features a unique rotational power system and many mechanical machines and tools.
  - **Connected** adds a variety of mechanical components and quality-of-life missing from the base mod.
  - **Steam & Rails** expands on the rail system with new bogeys, track types, and logistical blocks.
  - **New Age** integrates rotational power with electricity via a suite of coils, motors, solar panels and even nuclear reactors!
  - Many other Create addons help to fill out and expand the experience.
- **Immersive Engineering** forgoes the typical magic grey boxes with a set of beautiful, animated multiblocks and helpful gadgets.
  - **Immersive Geology** implements large ore deposits and an array of machines used to extract their precious resources.
  - **Immersive Petroleum** lets you pump crude oil from deep below ground and process it into diversely useful products.
  - **Immersive Technology** adds various miscellaneous machines, including novel power generation methods.
- **Ad Astra** expands the game's horizons to the entire solar system and beyond, with rockets, space suits and machines.
- **Alex's Caves** takes us underground to explore six beautiful, feature-packed new cave biomes.
- **Ars Nouveau** allows players to craft their own spells, create magical artifacts, perform rituals, and much more. 
- **Farmer's Delight** allows you to take a break from the demands of the factory and cook delicious food in a beautifully decorated kitchen.
- And many more.
